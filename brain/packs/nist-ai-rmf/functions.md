# NIST AI RMF — function playbook (concise)

**Source:** NIST AI RMF 1.0 Playbook categories (abridged).

## GOVERN

- Document AI policy aligned to legal and ethical obligations
- Define roles: executive sponsor, risk owner, model owner
- Integrate AI risk into enterprise risk register

## MAP

- Inventory AI systems with intended purpose and prohibited uses
- Map stakeholders and potential harms (safety, civil rights, privacy)
- Link datasets and third-party models to deployment context

## MEASURE

- Select metrics for accuracy, drift, bias, and security relevant to use case
- Run pre-deployment and periodic evaluations; retain evidence
- Track incidents and near-misses with severity taxonomy

## MANAGE

- Apply mitigations proportional to risk tier (human review, rate limits, kill switch)
- Monitor production signals; trigger rollback on threshold breach
- Communicate limitations to end users and downstream integrators
