# NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) — summary

**Source:** [NIST AI RMF 1.0](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) (NIST.AI.100-1).

## Functions

| Function | Goal |
|----------|------|
| **GOVERN** | Cultivate risk culture, policies, and accountability for AI systems |
| **MAP** | Contextualize risks across lifecycle, stakeholders, and impacts |
| **MEASURE** | Analyze, assess, and track AI risks with metrics |
| **MANAGE** | Prioritize and act on mapped/measured risks |

## Core characteristics (trustworthy AI)

- Valid and reliable
- Safe
- Secure and resilient
- Accountable and transparent
- Explainable and interpretable
- Privacy-enhanced
- Fair with harmful bias managed

## Control tags

- CSF:GV.OC
- SOC2:CC3.1
- 800-53:RA-3

## PGA harness fit

Crosswalk AI feature risks to GOVERN/MAP/MEASURE/MANAGE when running `risk-analyst` or `questionnaire` skills.
