# ISO 42001 control objectives (concise)

**Source:** ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Annex A (summary — not a substitute for the standard).

## Clauses mapped to operational practices

1. **Context & scope** — Define organizational boundaries for AI systems in production.
2. **Leadership** — Assign accountable owner for AIMS; approve AI policy annually.
3. **Planning** — Risk assessment before new model deployment; document acceptance criteria.
4. **Support** — Train builders and operators on responsible AI obligations.
5. **Operation** — Change control for prompts, models, and training data; monitor drift.
6. **Performance evaluation** — Internal audit of AI controls; management review quarterly.
7. **Improvement** — Track incidents, near-misses, and corrective actions.

## Evidence examples

- AI system inventory with owner and data classification
- Model/prompt change log with approver
- Bias/fairness evaluation report for high-impact use cases
- Human-in-the-loop procedure for consequential decisions
