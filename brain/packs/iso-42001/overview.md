# ISO/IEC 42001 — AI Management System (summary)

**Source:** [ISO/IEC 42001:2023](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html) — AI management system requirements.

## Purpose

ISO 42001 specifies requirements to establish, implement, maintain, and continually improve an **AI management system (AIMS)** within the context of an organization.

## Core themes (citable for questionnaires)

| Theme | Summary |
|-------|---------|
| **Governance** | Leadership accountability for AI policy, roles, and continual improvement |
| **Risk management** | Identify AI risks (bias, safety, privacy) and treat them proportionally |
| **Lifecycle** | Controls across design, development, deployment, monitoring, and retirement |
| **Transparency** | Document intended purpose, limitations, and human oversight |
| **Third parties** | Due diligence for AI suppliers and embedded models |
| **Evidence** | Maintain records demonstrating conformity to the AIMS |
