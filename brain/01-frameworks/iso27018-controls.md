# ISO/IEC 27018:2019 — PII Control Objectives

**Source:** Annex A, ISO/IEC 27018:2019 (extends ISO/IEC 27002 control structure)

Control objectives for PII processors in public clouds. Tag format: `ISO27018:A.<section>.<number>`.

---

## A.9 — PII processing in contracts and agreements

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.9.2.1 | Customer agreement | Process PII only per documented customer instructions |
| A.9.3 | Use of sub-processors | Disclose sub-processors; obtain customer authorization before changes |
| A.9.4 | Temporary files | Protect and erase temporary copies of PII |
| A.9.5 | PII disclosure requests | Inform customer of legally binding disclosure requests when permitted |

## A.10 — Information security in PII processing

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.10.1 | Audit trails | Record PII processing operations for customer audit |

## A.11 — Physical and environmental security

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.11.1 | Security requirements for PII | Apply physical controls where PII is stored or processed |

## A.12 — Operations security

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.12.3 | Backup | Protect PII in backups; align retention with customer agreement |
| A.12.4.1 | Audit considerations | Support customer audits within agreed scope |

## A.13 — Communications security

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.13.1 | Networks in PII processing | Segregate and protect networks carrying PII |

## A.14 — System acquisition, development, and maintenance

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.14.1 | Security in development | Apply secure development for systems processing PII |

## A.15 — Supplier relationships

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.15.1 | PII in supplier relationships | Extend PII obligations to sub-processors via contract |

## A.16 — Information security incident management

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.16.1.1 | PII breach notification | Notify customer of PII breaches without undue delay |

## A.17 — Business continuity

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.17.1 | Continuity of PII processing | Maintain availability per customer agreement |

## A.18 — Compliance

| Control | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A.18.1 | Compliance with legal and contractual requirements | Document compliance; limit PII return/deletion exceptions |
| A.18.1.4 | Return and deletion of PII | Return or delete PII at end of processing |
